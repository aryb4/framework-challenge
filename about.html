What we do

The Butcher's Fancy specializes in dry-aged beef, whole-animal butchery, house-made sausages, and custom cuts for dinner parties and events. We focus on traceability, sustainable sourcing, and old-school craft.

Catering & events

Small catering packages, live carving stations, and butchery demos available. Email or call for quotes and availability.
